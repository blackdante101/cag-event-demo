WELCOME TO CAG30

about this event

A Global Platform for Geoscience Collaboration

Welcome to the 30th Colloquium of African Geology (CAG30), a prestigious global event that brings together geoscientists, industry leaders, researchers, and policymakers to explore Africa's geological wealth.

Hosted by the Geological Society of Kenya (GSK) and the Geological Society of Africa (GSAf), this event marks 50 years of GSK and 60 years of CAG, promising an exciting convergence of expertise and innovation at Safari Park Hotel, Nairobi, Kenya.

Engage with key stakeholders from government, industry, research institutions, and academia. Build connections with geoscientists, entrepreneurs, and investors from around the world while exploring Kenya's rich geological heritage.
